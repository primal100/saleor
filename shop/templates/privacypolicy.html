Privacy Policy
This privacy policy has been compiled to better serve those who are concerned with how their Personal Information is being used online. Personal Information is any information that can be used on its own or with other information to identify, contact, or locate a single person, or to identify an individual in context. Please read our privacy policy carefully to get a clear understanding of how we collect, use, protect or otherwise handle your Personal Information in accordance with our website.

Who manages the information?

Nile Pottery is a partnership between individuals in Ireland and Uganda. The nilepottery.com e-commerce site is built and managed by Complete E-Commerce Solutions based in Dublin, Ireland. Data required for processing orders (names, addresses, e-mail addresses) is shared with our partners in Uganda.

Where is the information stored?

All non-payment information is stored on Google Cloud servers. Credit card information entered on the site is stored and processed by Stripe (Click here for the Stripe Privacy Policy). No information entered on the Paypal website is stored by this site. Financial details stored on Stripe or Paypal are not accessible to us.

What personal information do we collect from the people that visit out website?

When ordering or registering on our site, as appropriate, you may be asked to enter your name, email address, billing address, mailing address or phone number to help you with your experience and to process orders.

When do we collect information?

We collect information from you when you register on our site, place an order, fill out a form or enter information on our site.

How do we use your information?

We may use the information we collect from you when you register, make a purchase, surf the website, or use certain other site features in the following ways:

      • To process your orders, esnuring they are deliverd to the correct physical address.
      • To personalize your experience and to allow us to deliver the type of content and product offerings in which you are most interested.
      • To improve our website in order to better serve you.
      • To allow us to better service you in responding to your customer service requests.

How do we protect your information?

Your personal information is contained behind secured networks and is only accessible by a limited number of persons who have special access rights to such systems, and are required to keep the information confidential. In addition, all information you supply is encrypted via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology.

We implement a variety of security measures when a user places an order, enters, submits, or accesses their information to maintain the safety of your personal information.

All financial transactions are processed through a third party gateway provider (Stripe & Paypal). No credit card or other sensitive financial data are stored or processed on our servers.

Do we use 'cookies'?

Yes. Cookies are small files that a site or its service provider transfers to your computer's hard drive through your Web browser (if you allow) that enables the site's or service provider's systems to recognize your browser and capture and remember certain information

We use cookies to:

      • Help remember and process the items in the shopping cart.
      • Remember what user you are logged in as.

You can choose to have your computer warn you each time a cookie is being sent, or you can choose to turn off all cookies. You do this through your browser settings. Since each browser is a little different, look at your browser's Help Menu to learn the correct way to modify your cookies.

If users disable cookies in their browser:

We may not be able to keep you logged in as you browse our site, and preferences for logged out users, along with shopping cart information will not be remembered.

This does not include website hosting partners and other parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or serving our users, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential. We may also release information when it's release is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect ours or others' rights, property or safety.

Does our site allow third-party behavioral tracking?
We allow third-party behavioral tracking via server-side Google Analytics. This service may receive your IP address, information about which pages on our website you visit, and information about the browser you are using. The IP address can be used to identify your country of origin. Per-user data is not visible to our Ugandan partners, however they may be informed of general statistics (such as 20% of our visitors are from a certain country).
